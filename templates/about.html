About

Swinvest is an online platform for trading assistance and research automation, committed to providing investors with industry standard analysis of data, as well as stock projections and risk evaluations.

We also provide a live news feed which scrapes the web for the most relevant news, to give investors an understanding of a market.

Swinvest was created with the intention of helping new traders, allowing them to explore the stock market and get an insight into the world of investments. While the site is newbie friendly, it is industry standard, utilizing machine learning algorithms to project stock closing prices, as well as assess the risk of investment. We aim to be a tool to all users, regardless of background.
